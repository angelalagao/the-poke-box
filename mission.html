We believe people are entitled to delicious nutrition. There should be no need for dieting fads or counting calories. Only wholesome food made from fresh ingredients that leave you full, wanting more and most importantly feeling great!

Keeping true to the origins of poké, we proudly serve only the freshest cuts of fish, with a seasonal mix of fresh and seasoned ingredients that customizes every box to the flavor and nutrition profile all our customers.

The Poke Box delivers a product that is defined by each and every customer. Our mission is to serve our patrons with fresh, unique, responsible ingredients and allow everyone to enjoy poké their way.
